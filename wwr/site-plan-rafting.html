Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide an exhilarating and safe water rafting experience to our customers. We aim to create unforgettable memories for our customers while ensuring their safety and comfort.

Audience

Our target audience is adventure enthusiasts who enjoy outdoor activities and are looking for a thrilling experience. We cater to both individuals and groups, including corporate team-building events.

  • Question: What is the age limit for water rafting? Answer: The age limit is 14 years with parental consent.
  • Question: Do you provide safety equipment? Answer: Yes, we provide all necessary safety equipment and a safety briefing before each trip.
  • Question: Can we book a rafting trip for a large group? Answer: Yes, we cater to large groups. Please contact us for more details.
