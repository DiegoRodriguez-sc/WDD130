History

Whitewater Rafting was born on 1962 as a way to bring amazing experiences to intrepid people and share our love for river rafting with everyone! We began offering guided trips on weekends as a small business, but quickly a lot of adventure-seekers come to us looking for new challenges.

Nowadays we are in ten countries, as one of the most internationally recognized adventure companies. We have this user-friendly website to allow our customers to book trips online, access detailed information about our rafting adventures and share their breathtaking photos and feedback.
